🕌 Five Daily Prayers with Rakats

Steps of One Rakah (Prayer Unit)

  1. Niyyah – Intend the prayer.
  2. Takbir – “Allāhu Akbar”, hands raised.
  3. Qiyam – Recite Sūrah Al-Fātihah + another sūrah.
  4. Rukū‘ – Bow, “Subhāna Rabbiyal Adhīm” 3x.
  5. Standing again – “Sami‘allāhu liman ḥamidah”.
  6. Sujood (1st) – Prostrate, “Subhāna Rabbiyal A‘lā” 3x.
  7. Brief sitting – between prostrations.
  8. Sujood (2nd) – repeat.
  9. Tashahhud & Salām – after required rakats.

Conditions & Benefits

✅ Conditions Required

  • Clean body & clothes
  • Wuḍū (ablution)
  • Facing Qibla
  • Proper covering (awrah)
  • Correct prayer time
  • Intention (niyyah)

✨ Benefits of Salah

  • Strengthens connection with Allah
  • Brings inner peace & discipline
  • Removes sins
  • Builds patience & humility
  • Unites the Muslim community
